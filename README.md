# MediCaretaker
![Image of ASSIST-ME](https://github.com/VishwajeetJK/MediCaretaker/blob/main/homepage/MEDICARETAKER.png)

# This webapp is an attempt to provide users basic tools to maintain medical records, provide quick medication in absence of medical personnel and educate them about the importance of health insurance.

![Image of ASSIST-ME](https://github.com/VishwajeetJK/MediCaretaker/blob/main/screenshots/1.png)

# The application starts off with a quick introduction about why it is important to improve healthcare and empower rural population to acquire proper medical facilities.

![Image of ASSIST-ME](https://github.com/VishwajeetJK/MediCaretaker/blob/main/screenshots/2.png)

# Next comes the first tool that predicts the disease based on basic symptoms and recommend the medicine and proper dosage to tackle the situation temporarily.

![Image of ASSIST-ME](https://github.com/VishwajeetJK/MediCaretaker/blob/main/screenshots/3.png)

# After that comes the tool to predict the annual health insurance premium based on basic factors such as age, bmi etc. This tool can educate users about the importance of insurance especially for people under poverty line. 

![Image of ASSIST-ME](https://github.com/VishwajeetJK/MediCaretaker/blob/main/screenshots/4.png)

# Finally we have a dashboard where users can login and view their heath records whenever needed so that it can be used whenever medical facility is accessible.
